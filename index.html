Multiple Maths
How To Play!

Multiple Maths is a fun & interactive quiz that helps your child improve on their core maths skills by answering addition, subtraction, multiplication & division questions. You will have the option to choose which set of questions you would like to answer and the difficulty you would like to test your skills on. Here are the options available:

Types of maths quetions you can choose from:

  • Addition: a choice of addition based questions.
  • subtraction: a choice of subtraction based questions.
  • multiplication: a choice of multiplication based questions.
  • division: a choice of division based questions.
  • Multi quiz: a choice of all maths questions within one quiz.

Difficulty options you can choose from:

  • Easy: The questions you will have to answer will range from numbers 1 - 10.
  • Medium: The questions you will have to answer will range from numbers 11 - 25.
  • Hard: The questions you will have to answer will range from numbers 26 - 50.
