Multiple Maths
How To Play!

Multiple Maths is a fun & interactive quiz that was designed to help your child improve on their core maths skills by answering addition, subtraction, multiplication & division questions. Here is how to play!

  1. First pick your maths questions! This will determine what questions you will be asked during the quiz. The options are Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, Division and the final option is a mix between all maths equations for even more of a challenge.

  2. Next up you will have to pick the difficulty that you would like the questions to be. The options are:

    • Easy: The questions asked will range between the numbers from 1 - 10.
    • Medium: The questions asked will range between the numbers from 11 - 25.
    • Hard: The questions asked will range between the numbers from 26 - 50.

  3. You will then need to pick your Time Limit. The options are:

    • 1 Minute
    • 3 Minutes
    • 5 Minutes

  4. Lastly you will then need to pick your Questions Amount. The options are:

    • 12 Questions
    • 15 Questions
    • 18 Questions
